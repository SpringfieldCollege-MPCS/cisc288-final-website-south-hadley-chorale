Our History

The South Hadley Chorale was founded in 1984 as the South Hadley Community Chorus to celebrate Bach's 300th anniversary. Under the direction of the founder, Dr. Barbara Conant, Bach's Mass in B minor was performed in the inaugural year. we now reheare each year in October and, in March, perform with orchestra and soloists a concert consisting of one or more classical masterworks. Our members are drawn from South Hadly, Massachusetts and surrounding Pioneer Valley communities.

Rhearsals Info

Rehearsals for the 2022-2023 program year will start on Wednesday, October 12, 2022 at 7 P.M. at All-Saints Episcopal Church, 7 Woodbridge St, South Hadley with Alexandra Ludwig, our outstanding Music Director who is returning for her 5th year. Michael Rheault will also be returning as our accompanist.

Our dues for the 2022-2023 season are $75.00 which includes the cost of music.

You must bring proof of vaccination including the booster shot (unless not yet eligible-less than 6 months since 2nd shot). Masks must be worn at all times during rehearsal.
